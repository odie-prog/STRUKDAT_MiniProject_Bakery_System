# STRUKDAT_MiniProject_Bakery_System
Lina Fatima Azzahra Badr 5025251168

You are in-charge of running a popular walk-in bakery. You need to process customer orders in a way where customers who order "macarons" will always be prioritized regardless of order number, and orders for other baked goods are served as soon as possible after the macarons. The served goods are guaranteed to be fresh out of the oven.
